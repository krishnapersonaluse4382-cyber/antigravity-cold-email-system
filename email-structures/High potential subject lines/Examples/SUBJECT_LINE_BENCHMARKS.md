# Subject Line Examples & Benchmarks

This file showcases the practical application of the "Inner Circle" framework across different categories.

## 📈 Group 1: Engagement & Social Proof
*Frames the sender as a supportive observer.*
1. `Your reel got 1.2k likes, {{FirstName}}`
2. `{{FirstName}}, 1k people saved your video`
3. `Your story got 800 shares, {{Name}}`
4. `{{FirstName}}, your post got 1k comments`
5. `That video got 2k saves, {{FirstName}}`
6. `{{Name}}, 1.5k shares on your reel!`
7. `1.1k people shared your post, {{Name}}`

## 🤝 Group 2: The "New Follower" Entry
*Frames the sender as a genuine fan/follower.*
8. `Hi {{FirstName}}, your follower here!`
9. `{{Name}}, I've followed you!`
10. `Your new viewer here, {{FirstName}}`
11. `Hi {{Name}}, long-time follower!`
12. `Just followed you, {{FirstName}}!`
13. `New follower from {{City}}, {{Name}}`

## 🏠 Group 3: High-Intent Buyer/Inventory
*Frames the email as a direct business inquiry (Primal Desire for Success).*
14. `{{FirstName}}, what houses do you have?`
15. `{{Name}}, which houses are yours?`
16. `{{FirstName}}, what homes are available?`
17. `{{Name}}, which homes do you list?`
18. `{{FirstName}}, what listings do you have?`
19. `{{Name}}, what properties do you have?`
20. `{{FirstName}}, can I tour a listing?`
21. `{{Name}}, what listings do you have?`

## 👨‍👩‍👧 Group 4: Family Context
*The ultimate response trigger. Uses the "Care of Loved Ones" primal desire.*
22. `Hey {{FirstName}}, my mom liked your video`
23. `My dad saw your reel, {{Name}}`
24. `{{FirstName}}, my cousin sent me your post`
25. `Hey {{Name}}, my brother follows you`
26. `My sister liked your listing, {{FirstName}}`
27. `{{Name}}, my aunt sent me your video`

## 🏆 Group 5: Authority & Ego
*Triggers the desire to be "Superior" or recognized.*
28. `{{FirstName}}, why're you in the rankings!`
29. `Best {{industry}} ranking, {{Name}}?`
30. `You're in the top ranking, {{FirstName}}!`

## 🔄 Randomization Logic for Future Variations
To generate more, use this formula:
`[Relation/Metric] + [Action/Attribute] + [Context/PostType] + [Name]`
*Example:* `My Cousin (Relation) + Shared (Action) + Your Listing (PostType) + {{FirstName}}`
