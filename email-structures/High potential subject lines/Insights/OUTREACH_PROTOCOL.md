# Outreach Protocol: Assumption Control & Persuasion

## 🚫 The "Universal" Rule: No Activity Assumptions
Never assume what the client is *doing* (activity). Only reference what they are *experiencing* (outcomes).

### Forbidden Phrases (Activity Assumptions):
- "I noticed you're manually replying..."
- "Stop manually prospecting..."
- "You're spending too much time on..."
- "Your follow-up is slow..."

### Permitted Focus Areas (Outcome Problems):
- **Inbound Interest**: Lack of serious buyers.
- **Deal Flow**: Inconsistent or unpredictable revenue.
- **Revenue Leak**: Attention (views) not converting to dollars.
- **Burnout**: The pressure of managing every step of the growth system.
- **Time Freedom**: The desire to reclaim time and just focus on closing.

## 🎯 Persuasion Framework (The 1-2 Line Loop)
The body must bridge the Subject Line to a "Universal Pain" in under 50 words.

1. **Observation**: Start with a non-assumptive observation (the link on the profile/the engagement/the content quality).
2. **The "Stop Sign"**: Point to a technical or logic-based "Friction Point" that is universal.
3. **The Result (LF8)**: Offer the bridge to Success, Leisure, or Superiority.
4. **Low-Friction CTA**: Ask for a reply, not a call.

## 🏆 The "Controlled Growth" Authority Anchor
Use the following Roadmap elements to build credibility without being pushy:
- **The 90-Day Guarantee**: "Guarantees 2x more views in 90 days."
- **The 90-Second Diagnostic**: "Recorded a 90-second repair guide showing where the leak is."
- **Decision Elimination**: Framing the service as a system that removes uncertainty.
